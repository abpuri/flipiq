Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FlipIQ

How often is the data updated?
Our data is updated monthly when Zillow releases new research data. Our AI agents process this data within 24 hours and recalculate all opportunity scores. You'll be notified immediately when new high-potential opportunities emerge.
What markets are covered?
FlipIQ covers 26,000+ ZIP codes across all 50 US states. We analyze any market with sufficient data to calculate reliable opportunity scores. This includes most urban, suburban, and many rural markets.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! All plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime with a few clicks. If you choose an annual plan, you'll save 20% and can still cancel (though refunds are prorated).
Is there a free trial?
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to Professional features. No credit card required to start. You can explore the platform, receive alerts, and see the value before committing.
How is FlipIQ different from PropStream or DealMachine?
PropStream and DealMachine are search and filtering tools—you have to actively look for opportunities. FlipIQ is an intelligence platform with AI agents that proactively find opportunities and alert you. We analyze entire markets and predict where opportunities are emerging, not just show you what already exists.
What's included in the opportunity score?
Our proprietary score combines 5 factors: Appreciation (12-month price trend), Velocity (days to pending sale), Distress (% of listings with price cuts), Pricing Power (sale-to-list ratio), and Value Gap (spread between tiers). Each factor is weighted based on your chosen strategy.
