## Presenter Notes

### Slide 2 (Problem):
- Pause after "40+ hours" - let it sink in
- Ask: "How many hours do YOU spend?"
- Use their answer in rest of presentation

### Slide 5 (Scoring):
- This is where skeptics need convincing
- Explain each factor briefly
- Emphasize "backtested on real data"

### Slide 9 (Agent Monitoring):
- This is the differentiator
- Spend extra time here if interested
- Show live alerts if available

### Slide 13 (Pricing):
- Don't dwell on price
- Emphasize free trial
- Ask: "What tier makes sense for you?"
